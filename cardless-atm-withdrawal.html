How to Withdraw Money Using Cardless ATM Withdrawal

Published: January 2026

Use this guide to withdraw cash from an ATM without a physical card. Generate a withdrawal code, authenticate, and collect your cash securely.

What you'll need

  • Access to your account via a mobile app or online portal
  • A one-time withdrawal code or QR code (from your app)
  • A compatible ATM that supports cardless withdrawals
  • A stable internet connection on your phone

Withdraw cash using cardless ATM

  1. At the ATM, select Cardless or Cardless Withdrawal.
  2. Choose an authentication method: QR code or One-time code.
  3. For QR: open your app, select Cardless ATM, and scan the on-screen code.
  4. For code: generate a withdrawal code in your app, then enter your Phone number (if requested) and the Code at the ATM.
  5. Enter the amount you want to withdraw.
  6. Review details and select Withdraw or Proceed.
  7. Complete any additional verification (PIN or biometric, if prompted).
  8. Collect your cash and take the receipt.

Verify your withdrawal

  1. Confirm the amount on the receipt.
  2. Check your account balance reflects the withdrawal.
  3. Review your transaction history for the new entry.
  4. If applicable, check remaining daily withdrawal limits.

Troubleshooting

Code expired

  • Cause: One-time codes are time-limited.
  • Fix: Generate a new code and try again promptly.

QR code not scanning

  • Cause: Camera permission is disabled or glare obstructs reading.
  • Fix: Enable camera access, adjust the angle/brightness, or switch to the code method.

Cash not dispensed

  • Cause: ATM error or withdrawal limits exceeded.
  • Fix: Check your limits, try another ATM, and verify the transaction status in your account.
