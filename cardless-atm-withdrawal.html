How to Withdraw Money Using Cardless ATM Withdrawal

Published: January 2026

Use this guide to withdraw cash from an ATM without a card. Generate a secure code in your app, complete verification at the ATM, and collect your cash.

What you'll need

  • Access to your account via a mobile app or online portal
  • A one-time withdrawal code or QR code (from your app)
  • A compatible ATM that supports cardless withdrawals
  • A stable internet connection on your phone

Safety note: Do not share one-time codes or QR codes. Use them only at the ATM and within the validity window.

Withdraw cash using cardless withdrawal

  1. In the mobile app, open Withdraw cash.
  2. Select Cardless withdrawal and choose Generate code or QR code.
  3. If using a code: tap Generate code and keep it private.
  4. At the ATM, select Cardless withdrawal.
  5. For QR: scan the on-screen code in the app, enter the amount, and select Confirm.
  6. For code: enter your Phone number (if requested) and the one-time Code, enter the amount, and select Confirm.
  7. Complete any verification (PIN or biometric) and collect your cash and receipt.

Verify your withdrawal

  1. Confirm the amount on the receipt.
  2. Check your account balance reflects the withdrawal.
  3. Review your transaction history for the new entry.
  4. If applicable, check remaining daily withdrawal limits.

Troubleshooting

Code expired

  • Cause: One-time codes are time-limited.
  • Fix: Generate a new code in the app.
  • Fix: Use it promptly at the ATM.

QR code not scanning

  • Cause: Camera permission disabled or poor lighting.
  • Fix: Enable camera access in the app.
  • Fix: Adjust angle/brightness or switch to the code method.

Cash not dispensed

  • Cause: Withdrawal limit exceeded or ATM error.
  • Fix: Check your daily limits and try another ATM.
  • Fix: Verify transaction status in your account before retrying.
