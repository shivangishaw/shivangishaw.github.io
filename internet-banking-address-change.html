How to Change Your Address Using Internet Banking

Published: January 2026

Use this guide to change your address through internet banking. Enter your new details, verify them, and confirm the update.

What you'll need

  • Access to your internet banking account
  • Your new street, city, state, and postal code
  • Current contact information (email and phone)
  • One-time verification code (if required)
  • Reliable internet connection

Change your address

  1. Sign in to your account.
  2. Go to Profile or Settings.
  3. Select Address or Contact details.
  4. Enter your new address (street, apartment, city, state, postal code).
  5. Choose the address type: Residential, Mailing, or both.
  6. Review your changes and select Save or Update.
  7. Complete any verification (code sent by email or SMS).
  8. Confirm the update when prompted.

Verify your address update

  1. Open your profile and confirm the new address appears.
  2. Check for a confirmation message or email.
  3. Sign out and back in to verify the change persists.
  4. Review recent statements for the new address.

Troubleshooting

Address option not visible

  • Cause: The menu is located under Profile or Settings.
  • Fix: Search within settings or use in-app Help.

Verification code fails

  • Cause: The code expired or was entered incorrectly.
  • Fix: Request a new code and enter it promptly.

No confirmation received

  • Cause: Notifications are disabled or contact info is outdated.
  • Fix: Update email/phone and enable notifications.
