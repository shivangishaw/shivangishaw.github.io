How to Set Up a Meeting in Microsoft Teams

Published: January 2026

Use this guide to schedule a meeting in Microsoft Teams. Set the time, add participants, and share details securely.

What you'll need

  • A Microsoft Teams account
  • Teams desktop or web app access
  • Email addresses for invitees
  • Stable internet connection

Set up a meeting

  1. Open Microsoft Teams.
  2. Go to Calendar.
  3. Select New meeting.
  4. Enter a meeting Title.
  5. Set Start time and End time.
  6. Add Attendees by typing their email addresses.
  7. Choose location: Channel (for team meeting) or None (private meeting).
  8. Add an agenda or notes in Description.
  9. Select Send (or Save for later).

Optional settings

  • Repeat: set a recurring schedule.
  • Meeting options: manage lobby entry and who can present.
  • Channel: post the meeting in a specific team channel.
  • Registration: require registration for large sessions (if available).

Verify your meeting

  1. Confirm the meeting appears in Calendar.
  2. Check invitees received the meeting email.
  3. Open the meeting link to ensure it launches Teams.
  4. Join a test and check your microphone, speaker, and camera.
  5. If channel-based, verify the meeting post exists in the channel.

Troubleshooting

Invite not delivered

  • Cause: Incorrect email or blocked notifications.
  • Fix: Verify addresses, resend the invite, and ask recipients to check spam/junk.

Cannot join the meeting

  • Cause: Outdated app or sign-in issues.
  • Fix: Update Teams, sign in, or use the web app.

No audio or video

  • Cause: Device permissions or wrong device selected.
  • Fix: Allow mic/camera permissions; select the correct devices in settings; run a test call.
